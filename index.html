Biography

Kun Song is a Associate professor of ecology at East China Normal University. His research interests include vegetation science and plant ecolgy, especially for natural forests and urban area in eastern China.

宋坤,华东师范大学副教授，从事植被生态学研究，关注中国东部北亚热带自然森林与城市植被。

Interests

  • Vegetation Science
  • Urban ecology
  • ecological restoration
